# Providers

Provider coverage now includes Spain, Europe, USA and Global sources.

Spain:
- Banco de Espana, INE, CNMV, BME, Tesoro Publico, REE
- AEMET, Seguridad Social, Agencia Tributaria

Europe:
- ECB, Eurostat, OECD
- BIS, European Commission Data, EUR-Lex

USA:
- FRED, SEC EDGAR, BLS, US Treasury
- BEA, Census Bureau, EIA

Global:
- World Bank, IMF, CoinGecko, Stooq, Alpha Vantage, Finnhub, FMP, Twelve Data
- OpenFIGI, OpenCorporates, Polygon, UN Data
- RSS feeds for financial news

Freemium/API-key providers stay in the catalog but are skipped when their keys are not present.
